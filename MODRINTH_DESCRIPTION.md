# Spud Mod

A feet-themed Aether addon with a full progression system, tameable rats, custom fluids, and multi-mod crafting chains. Equal parts serious modded content and absurd comedy.

## What does this mod add?

### Chudington Ore
A glowing ore found only in the Aether dimension. Mining it drops Raw Spud — and has a 10% chance to spawn a hostile **Spud Rat** that wants to nibble you to death. Supports Fortune and Silk Touch.

### The Spud Progression
Raw Spud → Spud → Spudding → Spuddington → Spuddington Block

Each tier unlocks new recipes and capabilities. Spud is a fast-eating snack that grants **Gripper Feet** (speed + jump boost + wall climbing). Spuddington is a superfood with massive buffs.

### Spud Rats
Small hostile mobs that spawn from Chudington Ore. Kill them for **Toe Jam**, or tame them with Spud for a loyal companion that:
- Follows you and fights for you
- Rides on your head (invulnerable while riding)
- Sniffs out nearby Chudington Ore and marks it with particles
- Heals when fed Spud

### Gripper Feet Effect
A custom potion effect that gives:
- Speed (scales with level)
- Jump Boost (scales with level)
- Wall Climbing — walk into any wall and climb straight up like a spider

### Griptium — Endgame Material
A multi-step crafting chain requiring both Create and Ars Nouveau:

1. Toe Jam → **Create Crushing Wheels** → Crushed Toe Jam
2. Crushed Toe Jam + Lava → **Create Superheated Mixer** → Molten Grip Fluid
3. Molten Grip Fluid → **Create Compacting (Superheated)** → Grip Plate
4. Grip Plate + Spudding → **Ars Enchanting Apparatus** → Griptium

### Molten Grip Fluid
A pink lava-like fluid. Burns on contact. Can be bucketed, piped, and stored in tanks.

### Tools & Equipment
- **Griptium Pickaxe** — Only mines Chudington Ore (useless on everything else), but mines it fast with 50% bonus drops
- **Spuddington Block** — Wearable on your feet slot. Pure drip.
- **Spud Familiar** — Endgame Ars Nouveau item. Passively grants Mana Boost III and Mana Regen III while in your inventory.

### Throwable Raw Spud
Right-click to yeet a Raw Spud at your enemies. 2 damage on hit with a custom death message: *"Player was spudded to death"*

### Create Integration
- Crushing: Chudington Ore → Raw Spud
- Crushing: Toe Jam → Crushed Toe Jam
- Pressing: Raw Spud → Spud
- Mixing (heated): Raw Spud + Water → Spud
- Mixing (superheated): Crushed Toe Jam + Lava → Molten Grip Fluid
- Compacting (superheated): Molten Grip Fluid → Grip Plate

### Ars Nouveau Integration
- Enchanting Apparatus: Raw Spud → Spudding (500 source)
- Enchanting Apparatus: Grip Plate → Griptium (1000 source)
- Enchanting Apparatus: Spuddington → Spud Familiar (2000 source)

### Loot
All Spud items can appear in dungeon chests and loot containers (including Lootr). Rare items like the Griptium Pickaxe and Spud Familiar have very low drop rates.

### 20+ Advancements
Hidden achievements, puns, and challenges including:
- *"It's Chudding Time"* — Mine your first Chudington Ore
- *"I Got Spud On My Grippers"* — Wear a Spuddington Block as boots
- *"Ratatouille"* — Put a tamed Spud Rat on your head
- *"You've Been Chudded"* — Mine the ore with your bare fist
- *"Spud Gun"* — Hit something with a thrown Raw Spud
- *"Best Friends Forever"* — Tame a Spud Rat
- And many more...

### Item Tooltips
Every item has a funny description. Highlights include:
- Toe Jam: *"Dropped by Spud Rats. Don't ask where it comes from."*
- Griptium: *"Forged through feet, fire, and forbidden magic."*
- Spuddington Block: *"9 Spuddingtons compressed into pure drip. Wearable on feet."*

## Dependencies

| Mod | Required? |
|-----|-----------|

## Compatibility

Built for NeoForge 1.21.1. Designed to work in large modpacks (200+ mods). Recipes only load when their respective mod is present.
